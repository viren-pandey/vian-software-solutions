FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Clear answers about how we work, what we build, and what you can expect when you partner with Vian.

General

About Vian Software Solutions.

Vian Software Solutions is a software and digital services company that builds websites, custom applications, automation systems, and digital growth strategies for modern organizations. We work with startups, enterprises, banks, and government clients to deliver reliable technology solutions.
Vian operates globally with a remote team. We serve clients across multiple countries and time zones. Most project communication happens asynchronously through documented channels, with scheduled calls at key milestones.
Our core team includes developers, designers, and strategists with a combined experience of over four decades across web, software, and digital growth disciplines. We scale with trusted partners for larger projects when needed.
We work with startups, established businesses, banks, government organizations, and agencies across industries including finance, healthcare, education, e-commerce, and professional services. Each engagement begins with a discovery phase to understand domain-specific requirements.
Website Development

Building your web presence.

We follow a structured process: discovery and requirements gathering, information architecture and wireframing, design and content structuring, development and testing, review and revisions, and launch with post-launch support. Every step is documented and shared with the client.
A standard business website typically takes 4 to 8 weeks depending on complexity, number of pages, integrations, and content readiness. E-commerce and custom dashboard projects may take longer. We provide a timeline estimate during the proposal phase.
Cost depends on scope, pages, functionality, integrations, and design requirements. We provide fixed-price proposals with milestone-based payments. Typical business websites start from a few thousand dollars. Contact us with your requirements for a detailed quote.
Yes. We offer maintenance packages that include security updates, backups, content updates, performance monitoring, bug fixes, and analytics review. Maintenance is billed monthly or quarterly depending on the level of support required.
Software Development

Custom software and applications.

Ownership is defined in the project proposal. After full payment, all agreed deliverables including source code, documentation, and assets are transferred to the client, subject to third-party licenses, open-source dependencies, and retained pre-existing materials.
We use modern, widely-supported technologies: Node.js, Python, PHP for backend; React, vanilla JavaScript, and HTML/CSS for frontend; PostgreSQL, MySQL, and MongoDB for databases; and cloud platforms including AWS, Azure, Firebase, and Supabase. The tech stack is selected based on project requirements.
Security is integrated into our development process. We follow secure coding practices, implement authentication and authorization, validate inputs, handle data encryption where needed, and conduct security reviews before launch. Ongoing security monitoring is available through maintenance packages.
Yes. We design systems with scalability in mind, using modular architecture, efficient database design, and cloud infrastructure that can grow with your needs. We discuss expected growth patterns during discovery and plan accordingly.
SEO & Digital Growth

Growing your online presence.

SEO is a long-term strategy. Initial improvements are often visible within 4 to 8 weeks for technical fixes and on-page optimization. Significant ranking improvements typically take 3 to 6 months depending on competition, keyword difficulty, and domain authority.
We provide strategy and consultation for Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, Facebook, and Twitter. Our approach focuses on platform-specific content planning, audience targeting, analytics, and conversion optimization rather than generic posting schedules.
Yes. We provide regular reporting on key metrics including traffic, rankings, engagement, conversions, and ROI. Reports are tailored to your business goals and delivered monthly or quarterly with actionable insights and recommendations.
Payments & Billing

Pricing and payment terms.

We offer fixed-price project proposals with milestone-based payments, monthly retainers for ongoing work, and hourly rates for smaller scoped tasks. The pricing model is discussed and agreed upon during the proposal phase based on project requirements.
Yes. We issue professional invoices for all payments including GST-compliant invoices where applicable. Invoices include project details, payment terms, and transaction references. Receipts are provided upon payment confirmation.
No. All costs are clearly outlined in the project proposal before work begins. Any changes in scope are communicated and approved through a change-order process before additional work is undertaken. There are no surprise fees.
Support

How we help after launch.

Standard support requests are acknowledged within 24 hours on business days. Critical issues such as site downtime or security concerns are prioritized and addressed within a few hours. Response times are documented in the support agreement.
We provide support through email, a ticketing system, and scheduled video calls depending on the support package. Clients receive a dedicated contact and escalation path for urgent matters. Emergency contact information is provided for critical issues.
