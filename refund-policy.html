Refund Policy

This Refund Policy describes the terms under which Vian Software Solutions handles refunds, cancellations, and disputes for our professional services and project-based engagements. Please read this policy carefully before engaging our services.

Overview

At Vian Software Solutions, we are committed to delivering high-quality digital services that meet our clients' expectations. Because our services are primarily digital in nature and involve significant resource allocation from the moment a project commences, our refund policy is designed to be fair to both parties while protecting the value of the work we deliver.

This policy applies to all services offered by Vian Software Solutions, including web development, software development, mobile app development, UI/UX design, digital growth consulting, and automation solutions. Different refund terms may apply depending on the type and structure of the engagement.

Note: This Refund Policy is incorporated by reference into our Terms and Conditions. Capitalized terms used but not defined in this policy have the meanings assigned to them in our Terms and Conditions.

Digital Services

Because our services are digital in nature and involve the creation of custom deliverables, the following general principles apply:

  • Once work has commenced on a project, the time and effort invested by our team cannot be recovered. As such, refunds are calculated based on the proportion of work completed relative to the total project scope.
  • Any refund will be calculated after deducting the value of work already performed, any non-cancellable third-party costs incurred, and a reasonable administrative fee.
  • Clients are entitled to receive all deliverables produced up to the point of cancellation, even when a refund is issued for the incomplete portion.

Website Projects

For website development projects, the following refund terms apply:

  • Before Design Phase: If a project is cancelled after the deposit is paid but before any design or development work has begun, 100% of the deposit will be refunded minus any administrative processing fees.
  • During Design Phase: If cancellation occurs during the design phase, the Client is entitled to a refund of any amounts paid minus the value of design work completed, calculated at our standard hourly rate, and any third-party costs incurred.
  • During Development Phase: If cancellation occurs during the development phase, the refund is calculated based on the percentage of work completed. The Client will receive all work products produced up to the point of cancellation.
  • After Completion: Once a website project has been completed and delivered, all payments are final and non-refundable, subject to the warranty provisions in our Terms and Conditions.

Software Projects

Custom software development projects involve significant upfront planning and architecture work. The following refund terms apply:

  • Discovery and Planning Phase: Payments for the discovery and planning phase are non-refundable once the phase is complete and the discovery report has been delivered, as the value of this work is fully realized at delivery.
  • Milestone-Based Refunds: For projects structured with milestones, each milestone payment becomes non-refundable once the corresponding milestone is completed, delivered, and accepted by the Client.
  • Uncompleted Milestones: If a project is cancelled mid-milestone, the Client is entitled to a refund of the unused portion of that milestone's payment, calculated based on the actual time and resources expended.
  • Completed Projects: For fully completed and accepted software projects, all payments are final.

Milestone Payments

Many of our projects are structured with milestone-based payment schedules. The following rules apply to milestone payments:

  • A milestone is considered complete when the deliverables for that milestone have been delivered to the Client and the Client has had a reasonable opportunity to review them.
  • Once a milestone payment has been made and the milestone deliverables have been provided, that payment is non-refundable.
  • If the Client requests additional work beyond the original milestone scope, that work is billed separately and is subject to the same refund rules.
  • In the event of project cancellation, only the value of uncompleted milestones is eligible for refund, subject to the calculation method described above.

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable under all circumstances:

  • Domain name registration and renewal fees paid on behalf of the Client.
  • SSL certificate purchases and installation fees.
  • Third-party software licenses purchased for the project.
  • Hosting fees paid in advance to third-party providers.
  • Stock asset purchases (images, icons, fonts, etc.) made for the project.
  • API integration fees and第三方 service setup charges.
  • Administrative and processing fees (typically 5% of the refund amount or a minimum of INR 500, whichever is greater).
  • Retainer fees for the current billing period once the period has commenced.

Refund Process

To request a refund, the Client must submit a written request to support@example.com. The request must include the project name, a brief explanation of the reason for the refund request, and any supporting documentation. The following process will be followed:

  • We will acknowledge receipt of the refund request within 3 business days.
  • We will review the request and calculate any refund amount based on the applicable terms within 10 business days.
  • If a refund is approved, it will be processed within 15 business days of approval.
  • Refunds will be issued using the same payment method used for the original transaction, unless otherwise agreed.
  • For international payments, any currency conversion fees or bank charges will be deducted from the refund amount.

Chargebacks

We take chargebacks seriously and maintain a fair dispute resolution process. If a Client disputes a payment through their bank or payment provider without first attempting to resolve the issue with us, the following may occur:

  • We will suspend all ongoing work on the project until the chargeback is resolved.
  • We will provide the payment provider with all relevant documentation, including signed agreements, communications, and delivery records, to support our position.
  • If the chargeback is found to be unjustified, the Client will be responsible for all chargeback fees, administrative costs, and any collection expenses incurred.
  • We reserve the right to pursue legal remedies for fraudulent or abusive chargebacks.

We encourage Clients to contact us directly at support@example.com before initiating a chargeback so that we can resolve any issues amicably.

Exceptions

We recognize that exceptional circumstances may arise. We may, at our sole discretion, make exceptions to this refund policy in the following situations:

  • If we are unable to deliver the agreed-upon services due to circumstances within our control and after all reasonable efforts have been exhausted.
  • If there is a material breach of the service agreement on our part that is not cured within a reasonable time.
  • In cases of demonstrated financial hardship, where we may offer a partial refund or credit as a goodwill gesture.

Any exceptions to this policy must be documented in writing and signed by an authorized representative of Vian Software Solutions. No verbal agreements or representations regarding refunds are binding.
