What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, enhance user experience, and provide information to website owners. Cookies typically contain a unique identifier that allows websites to recognize your device when you return.

Cookies can be classified as session cookies, which are deleted when you close your browser, or persistent cookies, which remain on your device for a set period or until you manually delete them. They can also be classified as first-party cookies (set by the website you are visiting) or third-party cookies (set by a domain other than the one you are visiting).

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Operation: To ensure our website functions correctly, including page navigation, session management, and security features.
  • Performance and Analytics: To understand how visitors interact with our website, which pages are most popular, and how users navigate through our site. This helps us improve our website and services.
  • Preferences: To remember your preferences, such as theme selection (light or dark mode), language preferences, and other customization choices.
  • Marketing: To deliver relevant content and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. We use these cookies only with your consent.

Types of Cookies

We categorize cookies into the following types based on their function and purpose:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the basic functioning of our website.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand usage patterns and improve our site.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for targeted advertising and marketing measurement.

Below, we provide detailed information about each category of cookies we use.

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are necessary for our website to function properly. They enable basic functions such as page navigation, access to secure areas, and session maintenance. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

Examples of essential cookies we use include:

  • Session Cookie: A temporary cookie that maintains your session state as you navigate through our website. It expires when you close your browser.
  • CSRF Token Cookie: A security cookie that helps prevent cross-site request forgery attacks on our contact forms.
  • Theme Preference Cookie: Stores your theme preference to ensure consistent display across pages during your visit.

These cookies do not collect personal information and are necessary for the website to function. You cannot opt out of essential cookies, but you can configure your browser to reject all cookies. However, this may affect the functionality of our website.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies collect information about which pages you visit, how long you stay on each page, which links you click, and other usage patterns. This data helps us improve our website content, structure, and user experience.

The analytics cookies we use include:

  • Page View Tracking: Records which pages are visited and how users navigate between them.
  • Session Duration: Measures how long users spend on our website.
  • Referral Tracking: Identifies how users found our website (e.g., search engine, social media, direct visit).
  • Device and Browser Information: Collects anonymous information about the devices and browsers used to access our site.

The information collected by analytics cookies is aggregated and anonymized. We do not use analytics cookies to identify individual users. These cookies are set only with your consent.

Preference Cookies

Preference cookies allow our website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, personalized features. These cookies remember your settings and preferences so that you do not have to reconfigure them each time you visit.

Examples of preference cookies we use include:

  • Theme Preference: Remembers whether you have selected light mode or dark mode.
  • Cookie Consent Preference: Records your cookie consent choices so that you are not prompted repeatedly.

Preference cookies are non-essential and are set only with your consent. You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your cookies.

Marketing Cookies

Marketing cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns. These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners.

We use marketing cookies only when we have obtained your explicit consent. Currently, we do not actively use marketing cookies on our website, but we may introduce them in the future. If we do, we will update this Cookie Policy and seek your consent before placing any marketing cookies on your device.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services that we use to enhance our website functionality and analyze usage. These third parties may set cookies on your device when you interact with their services on our website. We do not control these cookies, and you should check the respective third-party privacy policies for more information.

The third-party services we currently use may include:

  • Analytics Services: We may use analytics platforms that set cookies to collect anonymous usage data. These cookies are subject to the respective analytics provider's privacy policy.
  • Font Services: We may use web font services that set cookies to track font usage and performance.

We select third-party providers who are committed to data protection and privacy. However, we recommend reviewing their privacy and cookie policies for detailed information about their data handling practices.

Your Choices

When you first visit our website, we display a cookie consent banner that allows you to choose which types of cookies you accept. You can:

  • Accept all cookies.
  • Reject non-essential cookies (essential cookies will still be set).
  • Customize your preferences by selecting specific cookie categories.

Your cookie preferences are stored in a cookie on your device. If you clear your cookies, your preferences will be reset, and you will be prompted again on your next visit. You can change your preferences at any time by clearing your cookies and reloading our website.

Managing Cookies

In addition to the controls provided on our website, you can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies on your device.
  • Block cookies from specific websites or all websites.
  • Block third-party cookies specifically.
  • Set preferences for cookie notifications.

Instructions for managing cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data.
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data.
Note: Please be aware that disabling or blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website and limit your ability to use certain features. Essential cookies cannot be disabled without impacting website functionality.

Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our use of cookies, legal requirements, or industry best practices. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this Cookie Policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
