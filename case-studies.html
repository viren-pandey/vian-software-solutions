Logistics & Transportation

Route Optimization for a Logistics Company

Challenge

A growing logistics company was managing delivery routes manually using spreadsheets. Drivers frequently encountered inefficient routes, missed delivery windows, and fuel costs were rising. Dispatchers spent hours each day planning routes and handling customer inquiries about shipment status.

The company needed a centralized system that could automate route planning, provide real-time tracking to customers, and give management visibility into fleet performance.

Solution

We built a web-based logistics management portal with an integrated route optimization engine. The system ingests delivery addresses, applies traffic data, and generates optimized routes. Drivers receive assignments through a mobile-friendly interface, and customers get automated tracking updates via email and SMS.

Node.js MongoDB Map APIs React WebSockets

Results

35%Reduction in fuel costs
50%Less dispatch planning time
98%On-time delivery rate
Real Estate

CRM and Lead Management for a Real Estate Firm

Challenge

A real estate firm with multiple agents was struggling to track leads across email, phone calls, and walk-ins. Deals were falling through the cracks because there was no centralized system to manage follow-ups, property showings, and client communications. Reporting was manual and inconsistent.

Agents needed a simple but powerful CRM that would help them stay organized without requiring extensive training or complex configurations.

Solution

We developed a custom real estate CRM with lead capture from multiple sources, automated follow-up reminders, property listing management, showing scheduling, and pipeline tracking. The system includes role-based access for agents, managers, and admins, along with a dashboard showing key metrics and deal stages.

Python PostgreSQL React REST API

Results

60%Faster lead response time
40%Increase in closed deals
100%Agent adoption rate
Finance & Accounting

Invoice Automation for a Service Business

Challenge

A service-based business was generating invoices manually in spreadsheets and sending them via email. The process was error-prone, time-consuming, and made it difficult to track payment status. Late payments were common because there was no automated follow-up system, and reconciling payments required manual effort.

The company needed an automated invoicing system that could generate, send, and track invoices while integrating with their existing accounting software.

Solution

We built an invoice automation platform that generates invoices from timesheet data, sends them automatically via email, tracks payment status, and sends reminder sequences for overdue payments. The system integrates with popular accounting platforms and provides detailed reporting on revenue, outstanding amounts, and payment trends.

Node.js MySQL Stripe API React

Results

90%Reduction in invoice generation time
25%Improvement in payment timing
0Manual data entry errors
Healthcare

Patient Portal for a Healthcare Provider

Challenge

A healthcare provider needed a secure patient portal where patients could book appointments, view medical records, communicate with doctors, and receive prescription refills. The existing process relied on phone calls and paper forms, leading to long wait times and administrative overhead.

The solution had to comply with healthcare data privacy regulations while being intuitive enough for patients of all ages to use.

Solution

We developed a HIPAA-compliant patient portal with appointment scheduling, secure messaging, medical record access, prescription management, and billing overview. The system integrates with the provider's existing electronic health record system and includes role-based access for patients, doctors, and administrative staff.

Python PostgreSQL React HL7 FHIR AWS

Results

70%Reduction in phone call volume
45%Increase in online bookings
99.9%System uptime
